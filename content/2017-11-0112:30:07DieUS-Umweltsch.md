Die US-Umweltschutzbehörde EPA zitiert die Bibel.On \...
========================================================

Date: 2017-11-01 12:30:07

[Die US-Umweltschutzbehörde EPA zitiert die
Bibel](https://www.buzzfeed.com/zahrahirji/epa-pruitt-new-science-advisors).

> On the journey to the promised land, \"Joshua says to the people of
> Israel: choose this day whom you are going to serve,\" Pruitt said.
> \"This is sort of like the Joshua principle --- that as it relates to
> grants from this agency, you are going to have to choose either
> service on the committee to provide counsel to us in an independent
> fashion or chose the grant. But you can't do both. That's the fair and
> great thing to do.\"

Man darf also die EPA nur noch beraten, wenn man kein Forschungsgeld von
ihnen kriegt. Damit sind die auf Drittmittel angewiesenen Klimaforscher
alle draußen, aber die steuerfreien Kirchen bleiben übrig.
